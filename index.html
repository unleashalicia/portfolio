Hi! I'm Alicia.

I'm a web developer with over-the-top enthusiasm for working on projects, learning how to do as much as I can get my hands on, and breathing new life into code.

Over the past several years, I have worn many hats, bringing care and expertise to the things I feel passionate about. I earned a master's degree in Creative Writing and moved to New York City to work in boutique literary agencies, discovering and strengthening great literary works and sharing them with the public. From there, I moved into the non-profit world, first as a Department Coordinator helping adults with developmental disabilities achieve their goals and then as a Case Manager at an emergency shelter for families experiencing homelessness.

In every industry, I have explored the edge of what is possible. As a web developer, I have a passion for innovation and creative problem solving. All of my applications tell a story and do their best to reach through the screen and affect their audience. I am a life-long learner and take pride in what I build and how it is shared with others. I look forward to connecting with you and getting started on new projects.
